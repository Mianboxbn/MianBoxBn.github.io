FAQs

Is there a delivery charge?

If you buy our noodle box more than $15 BND, there will be no delivery charge. But if less than $15 BND, then the charge will be $3 BND.

What time did you do your delivery?

We accept order from 9 am until 9 pm only.

How long does the delivery?

Depending on the area, since were located at Kiarong, it will probably takes about 15 - 20 mins to arrive, but if its other places it might takes about 30-45 mins.
(Depending on the order)
